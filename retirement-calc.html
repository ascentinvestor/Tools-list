How It Works

  1. Enter your current age, desired retirement age, and life expectancy.
  2. Input your current monthly expenses and any existing retirement funds you have.
  3. Set your expected rates for investment returns (before and after retirement) and inflation.
  4. The calculator instantly shows the total corpus you'll need and the monthly SIP required to reach it.

Key Assumptions

  • All rates (inflation, returns) are assumed to be constant over the period.
  • Investment returns are not adjusted for taxes.
  • Your lifestyle and expenses remain consistent, only adjusted for inflation.
  • The monthly SIP amount is assumed to remain constant.
